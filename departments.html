Mahendra Higher Secondary School Departments

Science Department

The Science Department at Mahendra Higher Secondary School is dedicated to fostering a passion for scientific inquiry and discovery. Our curriculum is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in various scientific disciplines, including physics, chemistry, and biology. Through hands-on experiments and engaging projects, students develop critical thinking skills and a deep appreciation for the natural world.

Management Department

The Management Department prepares students for leadership roles in the business world. Our programs focus on developing strategic thinking, effective communication, and problem-solving skills. Students gain practical knowledge in areas such as finance, marketing, and organizational behavior. With a commitment to ethical business practices, the Management Department equips students with the tools needed to excel in dynamic and competitive environments.

Humanities Department

The Humanities Department at Mahendra Higher Secondary School explores the rich tapestry of human culture, history, literature, and philosophy. Through engaging courses and thought-provoking discussions, students develop a deep understanding of the human experience. Our programs emphasize critical analysis, effective communication, and cultural awareness. Graduates from the Humanities Department are well-rounded individuals ready to contribute meaningfully to society.
